I design for the spaces between clicks.

Hi, I'm Parima — a UX designer and researcher with a particular interest in mixed reality, mental wellness tech, and education platforms.

My process is rooted in genuine curiosity: I want to understand why people do what they do, not just what they do. That means real conversations, careful synthesis, and a healthy skepticism of my own assumptions.

Currently exploring the intersection of embodied interaction and emotional design — and always looking for problems worth solving.
